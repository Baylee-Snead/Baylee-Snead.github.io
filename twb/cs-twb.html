The Whole Bean | Brand Identity & Landing Page Design

Objective

Create an entire brand identity for The Whole Bean that supports their brand persoanlity & messaging. They also needed a Landing Page for annoucning their Grand Opening and getting users to sign up for their emailing list.

The Problem

Develop a brand identity package to include brand style elements that support The Whole Bean's brand persoanlity. Create a landing page design that represents The Whole Bean's brand personality and that also encourages users to take two key actions: take note of and attend the shop's grand opening and sign up for the shop's emailing list.

The Solution

Developed a brand style guide that includes a color scheme, fonts, brand imagery, social media headers, and a logo that all support The Whole Bean's brand persoanlity. Designed a landing page using The Whole Bean's brand style elements that encourages users to attend the grand opening and sign up for the mailing list.
