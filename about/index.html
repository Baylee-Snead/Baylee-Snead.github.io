Hey, I'm Baylee, Welcome to My About Page

I'm a web & brand designer living in Pisgah Forest, NC, but I work with clients all over. I left my corporate IT job to start my own business and pursue a field I'm passionate about. Now I get to work with other awesome business owners to help them create a strong online prescense and branding they'll love.
 Throughout the design process, I'm very focused on the user's experience and how best to meet their needs. Afterall, the end goal is to attract your ideal clients and convert them in to paying clients. I love creating beautiful design solutions that do just that.
 When I’m not designing or coding, I’m out riding my mountain bike, shooting film with my drone, or traveling.
